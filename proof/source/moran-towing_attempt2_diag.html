How to Apply

Interesting, Challenging and Rewarding Job Opportunities

Moran is always on the lookout for capable, responsive and principled people who are seeking a place where they can shine. A family-owned company, we are committed to treating people fairly, providing ongoing development and learning opportunities, and offering generous health and employee benefits. If these things sound appealing to you, let’s get to know each other better.

Should you have a disability that requires assistance and/or reasonable accommodation with the job application process, please call the Human Resources Department at 203-442-2827.

Moran Towing Corporation, including its subsidiaries and affiliates, is an Equal Opportunity Employer and welcomes all qualified applicants. Applicants will receive fair and impartial consideration without regard to race, sex, color, religion, national origin, age, disability, veteran status, genetic data, or other legally protected status.
